\## Why I started this blog



Every developer I admire has one thing in common — they write.



Not because they have all the answers, but because writing forces you to actually understand what you know. You can fool yourself in your head. You can't fool a blank page.



So here I am.



\## What I'm working on



I'm Vipul — a developer currently building my skills in \[your stack here — e.g. Python, web development, APIs]. 

This blog is where I'll document everything I learn, build, and break along the way.



Expect posts about:

\- Projects I'm building and the decisions behind them

\- Things I learned the hard way

\- Tools and resources I find genuinely useful

\- Honest progress updates — wins and failures both



\## Why "building in public" matters to me



Most tutorials show you the clean version. The finished thing. The path that worked.



Nobody shows you the three hours spent on a config error, the Stack Overflow rabbit holes, the moment something finally clicks at 11pm.



That's what this blog is for. The real version.



\## What's coming next



My next post will be about setting up this very site — Hugo, GitHub Pages, and the errors I ran into along the way. 



If you're reading this and also just getting started — you're not alone. Let's figure it out together.
